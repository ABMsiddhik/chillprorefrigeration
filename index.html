Do you have questions?

How often should I service my AC or refrigerator?

We recommend servicing your AC and refrigerator at least once every 6–12 months. Regular service keeps cooling performance strong, reduces power usage, and helps avoid sudden breakdowns.

Why is my AC not cooling properly?

Poor cooling can be caused by dirty filters, low gas level, blocked airflow, or compressor issues. Our technicians can quickly inspect and fix the problem to restore proper cooling.

Do you provide original spare parts?

Yes, we always use genuine and high-quality spare parts to ensure safe operation, better efficiency, and longer life for your appliance.

Do your services include a warranty?

Yes, our repairs come with a service warranty. Warranty period depends on the type of repair and parts replaced, giving you complete peace of mind.

You can call us or book through our website. We offer clear pricing, quick response, and no hidden charges.
